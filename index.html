9- Progress Bar Animation

  1. Write a percentage in the field.
  2. The first bar will take the field's value as a width.
  3. The second bar will take (100% - the field's value) as a width.
  4. If the field's value is superior to 100 or smaller than 0 respectively the first bar's width will be 100% or 0%.

    16- Shuffle cards animation

    1. Enter a number between 0 and 5 as a distance.
    2. Click to add 25 cards(the button is clickable for once).
    3. Click on cards to shuffle them (the card you click go below all the cards).
    4. When you click on a card, It is recommended to wait for the animation to finish, and then click another card.
    5. Click to Fade Out and remove the cards, then you can do the steps from the beginning.
